Know how to spot the real offers

As with Black Friday, the vast majority of ecommerce sites communicate about this commercial period which takes place twice a year. But between the promotions that are advertised wholesale on sites and newsletters, and the real discounts you find on those sites, there can be a big difference.

For example, some brands inflate the price of products just before the official launch of sales discounts. That's why it's useful to compare a few days before the actual price of the item you want to buy to see if it actually gets a discount when the time comes. In addition, it is always useful to compare the price of the item on different online stores to take advantage of the lowest price during sales. Making a list can always help!
